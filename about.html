ABOUT US

Since 1992, ATS Group has built a reputation for innovation in the telecommunications and build industries.

We provide comprehensive expertise through working with mobile network providers and have hands-on experience in both legacy and emerging technologies. We are the industry's 'Go To' partner for complex, unusual, and difficult projects.

OUR RANGE OF SERVICES

Our design, manufacture, and aerial photography services are used by all industries. We offer specialized services across three core areas:

  • Telecommunications & Surveys: Elevated structural and panoramic surveys, line-of-sight checks, and drive testing.
  • Design & Engineering: Bespoke engineering, design and integration, and prototyping.
  • Aerial Photography & Footage: Promotional aerial photography and videography.
